The Year is 2012 and I am well into my belief that business are a system.

W. Edwards Deming

“Your business is a system.”
Deming started this conversation in NA.

Donella Meadows

“Your business is a complex system.”
Donealla Meadows is the matriarch of systems theory.
The book thinking in systems is required reading for any of us trying to grasp the complex nature of our companies.
Donella refines Deming.

Humble, Molesky, O'Reilly

“Your enterprise is a complex adaptive system composed of people who share a common purpose.”
The Lean Enterprise authors take it even further in refinement
People are the system
how are these people connected to that common purpose?

Woolley

“Your enterprise is a complex adaptive system that is composed of people who share a common purpose. The people are interconnected by the flow of information which is facilitated by communication and galvanized by trust.”
Interconnections in systems operate through the flow of information
People share information through communication
Communication quality is governed by trust and thus the efficiency and effectiveness of the info flow depends on trust.
distrust will be the number 1 cause of friction for information flow in any enterprise. When info doesn't flow... time is wasted

Global State

Set data-state="something" on a slide and "something" will be added as a class to the document element when the slide is open. This lets you apply broader style changes, like switching the page background.

State Events

Additionally custom events can be triggered on a per slide basis by binding to the data-state name.
